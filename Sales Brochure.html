Design, Installs & Service



Our engineers have decades of experience and factory training to help provide custom tailored application designsWith access to computer aided designs and a large library of past application experience our team is able to design the most efficient and affective lubrication systems quickly.


Lubromation employs full-time Installation Technicians as well as Service Technicians. Our techs are MSHA Certified and provide technical help with our lubrication systems. With our wide range of inventory, man-power, knowledge and tools we are able to complete an installation efficiently by the scheduled due date.


We also service existing systems that are problematic. Whether adjusting the system to run at its optimized level of accuracy or bringing an old un-used lubrication system back to life, we are equipped to handle any service call.


Repairs


We can repair your lubrication system components quickly and efficiently. No matter the brand or style. With our large inventory of parts for most major brands of pumps, hose reels, injectors, divider valves, timers and controllers, we normally get your repaired items back out to you in 7-10 days, of course if you are in an equipment down situation let us know and will assess the situation and come up with the best solution possible including providing a loaner pump if available.


We stock repair parts for Licoln, Balcrank, Farval, Alemite, Graco and others so that we can quickly repair your items. We also offer loaner pumps when available for our customers use during repair.
